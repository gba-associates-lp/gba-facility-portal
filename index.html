About Us

GBA Associates LP provides property and facility management services for the Defense Health Headquarters (DHHQ), ensuring operational excellence, compliance, and tenant satisfaction.

  • Comprehensive facility operations and maintenance
  • Vendor coordination and preventive maintenance
  • Compliance documentation and tenant support

Our team partners with DHA, WHS, and GSA to maintain a safe, efficient, and mission-ready facility for federal occupants across the DHHQ campus.
