Asset Leadership & Oversight

Dedicated Ownership and
Management of the DHHQ Campus

GBA Associates LP serves as the institutional property ownership entity for the Defense Health Headquarters. Our role centers on asset stewardship, maintaining operational continuity, and ensuring long term reliability across a mission critical federal campus.

Through direct coordination with federal stakeholders, we maintain performance alignment, continuity of operations, life safety readiness, and complete compliance with the multi-agency framework that governs the DHHQ environment.
