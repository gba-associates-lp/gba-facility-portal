About Us

GBA Associates LP is the property management team for the Defense Health Headquarters (DHHQ) complex. We work alongside the Defense Health Agency (DHA), Washington Headquarters Services (WHS), and the General Services Administration (GSA) to maintain secure, efficient, and reliable building operations.

  • Oversight of all facility operations and tenant services.
  • Coordination of maintenance, janitorial, and vendor activities.
  • Support for compliance, access control, and COI renewals.

From routine maintenance to large-scale project coordination, our team ensures uninterrupted support for mission-critical operations within the DHHQ campus. We work closely with building engineers, security, and DHA representatives to uphold operational excellence and federal compliance.

Our Services

We deliver full-spectrum facility management and tenant support services for the DHHQ campus.

Work Order Management

Processing, tracking, and verifying service requests for all maintenance and repair tasks through the Building Engines system.

Vendor Coordination

Managing vendor access, COIs, and scheduling for contractors performing work on-site across DHHQ buildings.

Facility Operations

Overseeing building systems, equipment, and preventive maintenance activities to maintain optimal performance and safety.

Access & Security Support

Assisting with PFAC badge requests, loading dock coordination, and secure entry processes for authorized contractors and staff.

Compliance & Documentation

Maintaining vendor compliance records, safety documents, and building permits to meet GSA and DHA standards.

Energy & Sustainability

Supporting building efficiency initiatives and sustainable practices throughout DHHQ operations.
